Grove Organic Farm CSA Membership: A Direct Connection to Fresh, Local Produce

Community Supported Agriculture (CSA)® is a model that connects farmers directly with consumers by allowing members to purchase a “share” of the farm’s harvest at the beginning of the growing season. In return, members receive a regular supply of fresh, organic produce throughout the season. The CSA model fosters a deeper connection between farmers and consumers, promoting transparency, sustainability, and local agriculture.
