Tour Our Farm

At Grove Organic Farm, the Johnson family believes in the importance of connecting people with the land and the food they eat. That’s why they offer a variety of engaging and educational farm tours, designed to give visitors a hands-on experience of sustainable farming practices. Whether you’re a local resident, a visitor passing through, or a school group eager to learn about where food comes from, the tours at Grove Organic Farm are a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the beauty and hard work behind organic farming.
