Our Story

The Johnson family’s connection to the land began generations ago, but it was only in the early 2000s that they transformed their family’s passion for sustainable farming into what is now known as Grove Organic Farm. Nestled in the rolling hills of a picturesque valley, the farm became a beacon for those seeking fresh, organic produce grown with care and respect for the environment.
